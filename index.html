About Us

ZillonLife is a young and vibrant ecommerce company with 4+ years in market that aims to provide good quality premium products at affordable prices.

  • We cater to Health & Wellness, Lifestyle, Air Purifier and Home Security buyers , Insurance , Financial Services , Internation Education, merchandise.
  • ZillonLife is bringing in the ZLF token as an immediate use token for its international product line – Online Education.
  • We see the Blockchain & Cryptocurrency market establish itself, moving forward, all new business lines as well as projects under

The ZillonLife umbrella will be driven by ZLF. Existing Business Verticals, Products and Services will also be migrated to have ZLF as an underlying token for transactions on the ZLF ecosystem.

  • ZLF tokens, which are the underlying token within the ZillonLife Ecosystem, will be Proof of Ownership tokens. One needs to hold minimum 1000 ZLF to qualify
  • 1% of monthly turnover will be paid as dividends to ZLF HODLERS
  • 10% of annual profits will be paid as dividends to ZLF HODLERS
