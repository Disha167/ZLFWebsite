Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why ZLF?

    ZLF is a TRON based currency in the Zillonlife ecosystem. Zillonlife has 30,000 customers across India and we are into 10 different product verticals.

  • What is the use case of the ZLF token?

    ZLF will be used in online tech education, merchandise, and blockchain-based talk show. The team is working on adding more product use cases.

  • Why should I hold ZLF?

    With ZLF, you can earn passive income. ZLF holders receive dividends every month. 1% of Zillonlife monthly turnover is paid to dividends holders, and 10% of the annual profit will be paid to holders.

  • What are future plans?

    Well, we have already ventured into the international market with online tech education and ZLF Talks. Future plans involve adding more useful product lines and transform businesses across the world.

  • Where do I hold ZLF?

    You can hold ZLF tokens in the Tron wallet. Use the Tron wallet app / Tron link extension in the browser

  • Where is ZLF traded?

    Currently, ZLF is being traded at NoleX exchange. Our team is already working on listing on other exchanges as well, and we are planning on announcing it soon.

  • Is there the ZLF community for the community?

    Yes, join us at telegram t.me/Zillonlife. You can also follow us on Twitter and Reddit
